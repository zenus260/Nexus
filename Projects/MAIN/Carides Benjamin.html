Our Directors

Telis Carides

After obtaining his first degree in physiology and genetics, he pivoted to complete a post-graduate LLB so as to pursue his interest in civil law. Telis’ LLB was peppered with numerous extra courses in the schools of law, economics and molecular and cell biology, graduating with multiple academic awards.

In January 2021, Telis simultaneously commenced his articles at a prominent South African property law firm and an LLM in the field of business and commercial law on a part-time basis. During his articles, Telis specialised in commercial, retail and industrial property law, a field which cemented his passion for all contract and property related law. His LLM focused on cross-border agreements and tax.

Telis was admitted as an attorney in 2023, and a notary in 2024. In these roles, he has become a well-respected practitioner, litigating at trial, conducting in-house training and media interviews. Telis has represented South Africa’s largest listed and unlisted landlords in both property transfers and litigation – and assisted in drafting South Africa’s most widely used residential lease agreement. He routinely leads arguments out of both Magistrates’ Courts throughout Gauteng, and the High Court of South Africa.

Micaela Benjamin

Micaela’s academic prowess was apparent from her schooling years, where she matriculated amongst the top achievers in South Africa. Having graduated with a BSc in physiology and biochemistry from Wits (wherein she was awarded the prize of top chemistry student), she went on to complete a year in medicine before finding her passion in civil law.

In 2021, Micaela obtained her LLB degree, again with numerous academic awards – including Dean’s List in each year, and multiple merit awards. In January 2022, Micaela commenced articles at a specialist property law firm while studying towards her LLM degree with a focus on advanced contracts and tax law.

Micaela was simultaneously admitted as an attorney, notary and LLM graduate. Backed by her exemplary academic foundation, Micaela is regarded as a residential eviction specialist, having routinely obtained favourable outcomes for her clients, often under challenging circumstances. She is however well-versed in the areas of both commercial and residential property disputes, attending to urgent applications, and routinely running opposed motions.
